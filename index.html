ABOUT

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science under the supervision of Dr. Adam Doupé at Arizona State University . I am a member of the Laboratory of Secure Engineering for Future Computing (SEFCOM) which is directed by Dr. Gail-Joon Ahn , Dr. Adam Doupé and Dr. Ziming Zhao.

Research

My research interests are computer networks, automated systems, human-computer interaction, education through serious game development. Currently, I am working on a qualitative study where I am conducting interviews with professionals from various organizations with a goal of improving the reliability and robustness of their internal networks.
