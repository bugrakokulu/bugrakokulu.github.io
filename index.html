ABOUT

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science under the supervision of Dr. Adam Doupé at Arizona State University. I am a member of the Laboratory of Secure Engineering for Future Computing (SEFCOM), which is directed by Dr. Gail-Joon Ahn, Dr. Adam Doupé, Dr. Yan Shoshitaishvili, Dr. Tiffany Bao, and Dr. Ruoyu (Fish) Wang.

Research

My research interests are in the areas of Usable Security & Privacy. Specifically, I study the human component of security by using qualitative and quantitative research methods.
